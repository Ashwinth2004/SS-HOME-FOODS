Our Story

From Our Kitchen To Your Table

SS Home Foods began with one simple belief — everyone deserves a proper, healthy, home-cooked meal, even when living away from home. We bring the warmth of Namma Veetu Samayal to students, working professionals, families and senior citizens across Anna Nagar West Extension.
