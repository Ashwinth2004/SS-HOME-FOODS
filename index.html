Our Story

From Our Kitchen To Your Table

SS Home Foods began with one simple belief — everyone deserves a proper, healthy, home-cooked meal, even when living away from home. We bring the warmth of Namma Veetu Samayal to students, working professionals, families and senior citizens across Anna Nagar West Extension, serving fresh every day from Monday to Saturday.

  • Authentic Tamil Nadu RecipesSambar, rasam, chettinad curries, chapati, kurma and traditional banana-leaf thali meals.
  • Hygienic Home KitchenSmall-batch cooking with quality oil, fresh vegetables and zero preservatives — every single day.
  • Veg Daily, Non-Veg on WednesdaysWholesome veg meals every day; special non-veg dishes served at lunch and dinner on Wednesdays.
  • Flexible PlansOrder a single meal in advance, or subscribe for the week or full month and save more.
